Yishu Zhou (周祎舒)

I am an optical engineer at the Microsoft Azure Networking team, exploring the next generation of optical networks for AI and high-performance computing.
I completed my PhD in Applied Physics at Yale University, under the guidance of Prof. Peter Rakich. My PhD research is primarily centered around integrated photonics, microwave photonics, acoustics, and quantum nonlinear optics. Specifically, I delve into: i) Harnessing optical nonlinearities and light-matter interactions to pioneer the development of state-of-the-art integrated photonic and microwave photonic devices; ii) Designing innovative integrated photonic systems to advance our understanding of nonlinear optics and quantum optomechanics.
Before joining Yale, I completed my undergraduate studies in Huazhong University of Science and Technology, where my research focused on wearable energy harvesting devices.
